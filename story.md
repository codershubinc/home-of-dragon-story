# Legacy of Emperor: Under the Draconic Wing

**Complete Narrative Overview**

---

## Act I: The Spark (Days 1-10)

**"The Shadow Falls"**

The game begins at the **Black Spire**, the capital of the Empire. For 30 years, the world has been silent, living in fear of the Empire's dragons after the disappearance of the legendary "World-Breaker" dragon, Balar.

**The Incident:**
During the "Feast of Unity," intended to keep the vassal kingdoms in line, **Emperor Tyros** chokes to death on poisoned wine. In the chaos, **Lord Vorian** (the Emperor's Ward and general) seizes the castle. He does not claim the throne for himself yet; instead, he acts as "Protector of the Realm."

**The Frame:**
Vorian accuses **King Malik** (Desert Kingdom) of the murder. He orders the execution of the Desert Prince, **Altair** (The Player).

- **Gameplay:** You must navigate the castle dungeons, retrieve your daggers, and reach the Dragon Pit.
- **The Escape:** You bond with **Nightshade** (a small, fast dragon) and escape the Spire in a high-speed aerial chase against Imperial Guards.

---

## Act II: The Gathering Storm (Days 11-45)

**"Building the Rebellion"**

Back in the **Desert Kingdom**, King Malik is wounded defending the borders. He gives Altair a mission: _The Empire has 5 Dragons. We have 2. We cannot win alone. You must unite the Four Pillars._

**The Quest Loops:**

1.  **The Steel Kingdom (North-West):**
    - _Problem:_ King Hrothgar refuses to fight because he fears dragon fire.
    - _Solution:_ You must help his engineers perfect the "Scale-Piercer" Ballista by luring a wild wyvern into their firing range.
2.  **The Snow Kingdom (North-East):**
    - _Problem:_ The Frost Giants are asleep.
    - _Solution:_ You must fly into the "Blizzard Zone" (where engines freeze) and wake the Giant Elder.
3.  **The Island Kingdom (South):**
    - _Problem:_ The Imperial Navy has blockaded their ports.
    - _Solution:_ Use Nightshade to dive-bomb the supply ships at night, breaking the blockade.
4.  **The Forest Kingdom (The Betrayal):**
    - _Twist:_ When you arrive, you find they are loyal to Vorian. You are ambushed by **Prince Cassius** (Imperial Spy) and his Acid-Dragon, **Venin**. This is the first major Boss Fight.

**The Spy Network:**
Throughout this Act, you receive letters from **Princess Elara** inside the Black Spire. She reveals Vorian is searching for something called "The Titan's Roost."

---

## Act III: The World-Breaker (Days 46-60)

**"The End of the Age"**

Vorian grows desperate. His "Aura" becomes terrifying. He unleashes **Vorgath (The Eclipse)** to burn the Steel Kingdom to the ground as a warning.

**The Discovery:**
Altair realizes Vorian isn't just trying to rule; he is trying to find **Balar** (The missing 8th Dragon) to become a God.

- **The Race:** You must fly to the "Titan's Roost"—a hidden volcanic island inside a perpetual storm—before Vorian does.

**The Climax:**
Inside the storm, you find **Balar**. He is colossal—a mountain with wings.

- **The Final Choice:**
  1.  **Ending A (The Breaker):** You wake Balar. He destroys the Empire's fleet instantly but then turns on the world. You must kill Vorian, then kill Balar to end the age of dragons forever.
  2.  **Ending B (The New Titan):** You defeat Vorian in a duel. You bond with Balar. You fly back to the Black Spire as the new, terrifying Emperor. The cycle continues.
