# start

**Phase:** Prologue & Tutorial
**Location:** The Black Spire (Imperial Capital)
**Time:** Sunset (The "Golden Hour" turning into Night)

---

## Scene 1: The Walk of Submission (Tutorial - Movement & Aura)

**Objective:** Walk from the Dragon Landing Pad to the Great Hall.

**Visuals:**

- **The Camera:** Starts looking at the ground (boots on black stone). Pan up to reveal the massive **Black Spire** fused with the volcano.
- **The Scale:** In the background, **Vorgath (The Eclipse)** is sleeping on a tower. His tail hangs down, and it is larger than the entire walkway the player is standing on.
- **NPCs:** Imperial Guards in heavy black plate armor line the path. They do not move.

**Gameplay Events:**

1.  **Movement Control:** Player gains control of **Prince Altair**.
    - _Prompt:_ "Use WASD to Move."
2.  **The Aura Introduction (Vorian's Entrance):**
    - As you walk, **Lord Vorian** walks past you in the opposite direction, flanked by two elite guards.
    - _System Check:_ The screen edges blur grey. The audio muffles (tinnitus ringing). Altair's walking speed automatically slows down by 30%.
    - _Dialogue:_ Vorian pauses but doesn't look at you.
      > **Vorian:** "Keep your head down, sand-rat. The air is thin up here."
    - _Effect:_ Once he passes, the color returns to the screen. This teaches the player: **Vorian = Danger.**

3.  **The Father's Warning:**
    - You reach the Great Door. **King Malik** (your father) is waiting.
    - **Interaction:** Press 'E' to talk.
      > **King Malik:** "Do not speak unless spoken to, Altair. They are looking for a reason to burn our dunes to glass. Just survive the night."

---

## Scene 2: The Feast of Treason (Cinematic & Dialogue)

**Objective:** Navigate the political tension of the dinner.

**Setting the Scene:**

- A long obsidian table.
- **Head of Table:** Emperor Tyros (looking sick/pale).
- **Right Hand:** Lord Vorian (polishing an apple with a dagger).
- **Left Hand:** Princess Elara (looking anxious).
- **Guests:** The Kings of Steel, Snow, and Desert.

**The Scripted Event:**

1.  **The Toast:** Emperor Tyros stands up shakily to propose a toast to "Eternal Unity."
2.  **The Poison:** He drinks. He coughs. The cough becomes violent. He collapses, foaming at the mouth.
3.  **The Chaos:**
    - The music stops instantly.
    - **Lord Vorian** slams his hand on the table.
    - **Vorian:** "TREASON! The Desert King has poisoned the wine!"
4.  **The Frame-Up:**
    - Guards grab King Malik.
    - Vorian points his sword at **Altair**.
    - **Vorian:** "Take the boy to the dungeons. I will flay the truth out of him myself."

---

## Scene 3: The Dungeon Breakout (Tutorial - Stealth & Combat)

**Objective:** Escape the cell and retrieve your gear.

**Gameplay Loop:**

1.  **The Cell:** Altair is thrown into a dark cell.
    - _Mechanic:_ "Hidden Blade." Altair reveals he still has a small jagged piece of metal/glass.
2.  **The Lure:** A guard walks by.
    - _Prompt:_ "Press 'F' to Whistle."
    - The guard investigates.
    - _Prompt:_ "Press 'Left Click' to Takedown." (Animation: Pull guard through bars, knock him out).
3.  **Stealth Section:**
    - You are weaponless (except for the guard's short sword).
    - You must sneak past 3 patrols to reach the **Evidence Room**.
    - _Mechanic:_ **Crouch (Ctrl)** reduces noise radius.
4.  **Gear Retrieval:**
    - Find your **Twin Daggers** and **Recurve Bow** in a chest.
    - _Equip Animation:_ Altair spins the daggers. "Now it's a fair fight."

---

## Scene 4: The Dragon Pit (Platforming & Bonding)

**Objective:** Reach Nightshade before the guards kill him.

**Level Design:**

- A vertical shaft with chains and cages. You must parkour up.
- **Visual:** You see **Nightshade** (your small black dragon) muzzled and chained in the center. Imperial Beastmasters are hitting him with pikes.

**The Action:**

1.  **Combat:** Jump down and kill the 2 Beastmasters using the **Air Assassination** skill (Introduction to vertical combat).
2.  **The Unchaining:**
    - _Interaction:_ Rapidly press 'E' to break the chains.
    - **Nightshade** roars and nudges Altair. The bond is re-established.
    - _UI Update:_ **Dragon Link: Active.**

---

## Scene 5: The First Flight (High-Speed Escape)

**Objective:** Escape the Black Spire airspace.

**Gameplay Loop (The "Hook"):**

1.  **Takeoff:** You mount Nightshade.
    - _Prompt:_ "Hold 'Space' to Flap/Ascend."
    - You burst out of the cave into the open sky. It is now night, raining heavily.
2.  **The Pursuit:**
    - **Prince Cassius** appears on **Venin** (Green Dragon).
    - **Cassius:** "Nowhere to run, little prince!"
    - _Mechanic:_ **Venin spits acid.** You must use 'Q' and 'E' to barrel roll and dodge the green projectiles.
3.  **The Hyper-Sonic Dive:**
    - You cannot fight Venin (he is too big).
    - **Mission Goal:** Dive into the "Cloud Layer" below to break line of sight.
    - _Mechanic:_ "Press 'Shift' to Tuck Wings."
    - **Speed Effect:** The camera shakes violently, wind lines appear (anime style), sound goes mute for a second as you break the sound barrier.

**Ending the Prologue:**

- You lose Cassius in the storm clouds.
- Altair and Nightshade glide silently over the **Sand Dunes** of the Desert Kingdom border.
- **Title Card Drops:**
  > **LEGACY OF EMPEROR: UNDER THE DRACONIC WING**
