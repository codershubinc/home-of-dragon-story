# story_start.md

**Project:** Legacy of Emperor: Under the Draconic Wing
**Phase:** Day 1 Development - The Prologue
**Scene:** 01 - The Walk of Submission
**Location:** The Black Spire - Outer Bridge
**Time of Day:** Sunset (Volcanic/Red Hue)

---

## 1. Scene Overview & Emotional Goal

**The Goal:** To make the player feel physically small and politically powerless.
**The "So What":** This scene establishes the "Aura" mechanic immediately. Before a single sword is drawn, the player learns that **Lord Vorian** is a terrifying force of nature, and that dragons are massive, looming threats, not just pets.

---

## 2. Environment & Asset Blockout (Blender -> UE5)

### A. The Bridge (The Walkway)

- **Asset Name:** `SM_Bridge_Blockout`
- **Dimensions:** 10m Wide x 150m Long.
- **Design:**
  - No guard rails (implies danger).
  - Massive obsidian pillars every 20m.
  - **Texture/Material:** Rough, dark stone (use Quixel Bridge "Volcanic Rock").

### B. The Black Spire (The Backdrop)

- **Asset Name:** `SM_Spire_Main`
- **Scale:** **Massive.** It must occupy 60% of the screen vertical space.
- **Shape:** A cylinder fused with a jagged mountain cone.
- **Placement:** The bridge leads directly into a small "Maw" (gate) at the base of this massive structure.

### C. Vorgath (The Background Threat)

- **Asset Name:** `SM_Dragon_Vorgath_Proxy`
- **Placement:** Perched on a rock outcropping ~60m above the bridge.
- **The Tail:** His tail hangs down _below_ the bridge level, swaying slowly.
- **Scale:** The tail alone should be thicker than the player's body.

---

## 3. Lighting & Camera (The "Aura" Setup)

### Camera Settings (`BP_ThirdPersonCharacter`)

To achieve the "House of the Dragon" sense of scale:

- **CameraBoom Length:** `500` (Pull camera back).
- **Camera Socket Offset:** `Z = -40` (Lower camera to waist height).
- **Camera Lag:** `Enabled` (Speed 10.0). _This makes the movement feel heavy._
- **Field of View (FOV):** `100` (Widens the view to show the Spire's height).

### Global Atmosphere

- **Directional Light:** Low angle (Sunset). Color: Deep Orange (`R:1.0, G:0.2, B:0.0`).
- **Volumetric Fog:** **Enabled.**
  - _Density:_ `0.05`.
  - _Color:_ Dark Grey/Red.
  - _Effect:_ Creates "God Rays" coming through the dragon’s tail and the pillars.

## 4. The Core Mechanic: Lord Vorian's "Aura"

**Logic Name:** `BP_Aura_Trigger_Vorian`
**Type:** Actor Blueprint with Box Collision.

### A. The Components

1.  **Box Collision:** Size `(X: 800, Y: 2000, Z: 500)`. Covers the width of the bridge.
2.  **PostProcessComponent:**
    - **Priority:** `10`.
    - **Blend Weight:** `0.0` (Starts invisible).
    - **Settings:**
      - _Saturation:_ `0.2` (Desaturate world).
      - _Vignette:_ `0.8` (Darken edges).
      - _Film Grain:_ `0.5` (Add visual noise/stress).
      - _Color Grading (Temp):_ `Cool/Blue` (Contrast with the red sunset).

### B. The Blueprint Logic (Event Graph)

1.  **Event:** `OnComponentBeginOverlap (Player)`.
2.  **Timeline Node:** "FadeInAura" (Length: 1.5 seconds).
    - _Track:_ Float (0 to 1).
    - _Update:_ Set **PostProcessComponent -> Blend Weight**.
3.  **Movement Penalty:**
    - Cast to `ThirdPersonCharacter`.
    - Get `CharacterMovement`.
    - Set **MaxWalkSpeed** to `200` (Forced Walk).
4.  **Audio Cue:**
    - Play Sound 2D: `SFX_Heartbeat_Thump`.
    - Play Sound 2D: `SFX_HighPitch_Ringing` (Low volume).

## 5. Scripted Sequence (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: The Introduction

- **Player Control:** Limited (Walk only).
- **Visual:** Player walks forward. The shadow of Vorgath’s tail sweeps across the bridge.
- **Ambient Sound:** Wind howling, distant dragon growls (low frequency rumble).

### Step 2: The Encounter

- **Trigger:** Player hits the `BP_Aura_Trigger_Vorian` halfway across the bridge.
- **Action:**
  - **Lord Vorian** (NPC) walks past the player on the left.
  - He does not stop walking. He stares straight ahead.
  - _Aura Effect triggers:_ Screen goes grey, heartbeat sound plays.
  - **Dialogue (Subtitle Only):**
    > **Vorian:** "Keep your head down, sand-rat. The air is thin up here."

### Step 3: The Father

- **Location:** End of the bridge, near the Great Gate.
- **NPC:** **King Malik**.
- **Trigger:** Player enters a small trigger box near Malik.
- **Interaction:** Press 'E'.
- **Cinematic Camera:** Switch to a close-up "Over the Shoulder" shot.
- **Dialogue:** > **King Malik:** "Do not speak unless spoken to, Altair. They are looking for a reason to burn our dunes to glass. Just survive the night."
